I am a postdoc assistant in the group of Monika Ludwig at TU Vienna. Before that I was a PhD student under the supervision of Martin Henk. My research interests include

- Convex geometry,

- Geometry of numbers,

- Ehrhart theory,

- Spherical and hyperbolic geometry.
