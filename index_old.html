Ansgar Freyer

I currently work at FU Berlin as a principal investigator in the project Extremal bodies with respect to lattice functionals together with Gennadiy Averkov, Giulia Codenotti and Kyle Huang. This project is part of the SPP Combinatorial Synergies.

Before that I was a postdoc in Monika Ludwig's group at TU Wien. And before that I was a PhD student under the supervision of Martin Henk at TU Berlin.

Research Interests

My research revolves around discrete questions in convexity, mostly (but not exclusively) involving lattice points. More specifically, I've been working on:

  • Discrepancy of volume and lattice point enumerator,
  • Ehrhart theory and valuations of lattice polytopes,
  • Volume distribution in polytopes,
  • Polarity (in particular in the context of width and volume).
