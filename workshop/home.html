Modern Perspectives on Geometry of Numbers

September 09 - 12, 2025, Cottbus

The conference is about the role of Minkowski's geometry of numbers in various branches of modern mathematics, such as toric geometry, energy minimization and convexity. In addition to the research talks, there will be two mini courses on toric geometry and the MM*-estimate in geometry of numbers.
