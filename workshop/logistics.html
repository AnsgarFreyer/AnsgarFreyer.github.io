Getting to Cottbus

By plane. The closest airport to Cottbus is BER in Berlin. From there it's a 75 minute train ride to Cottbus. Train tickets can be purchased here. Smaller airports close to Cottbus are in Leipzig/Halle (LEJ) and Dresden (DRS).

By train. You can find all information on connections and tickets on DB webpage.
