Getting to Cottbus

By plane. The closest airport to Cottbus is BER in Berlin. From there it's a 75 minute train ride to Cottbus. Train tickets can be purchased here. Smaller airports close to Cottbus are in Leipzig/Halle (LEJ) and Dresden (DRS).

By train. You can find all information on connections and tickets on DB webpage.

Venue

The talks and lectures will take place in Zentrales Hörsaal Gebäude of BTU Cottbus, Hörsaal C. It's located at Platz der Deutschen Einheit 1.

Conference Dinner

The conference dinner will take place on Tuesday evening at 7pm at Spreewehrmühle. Food and a (non-alcoholic) drink are covered for registered participants.
